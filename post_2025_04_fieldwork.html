BioMonI fieldwork - Azores (Portugal)

Between June 20th and November 29th 2024, the Azorean team resampled 12 permanent plots, placed and first sampled in 2012, at 6 altitudinal levels (from 0 to 1000 m a.s.l.). We resampled a total of 12 (10 m x 10 m) plots (divided in four, 25 m2, subplots). For vascular plants, we measured: - Percent coverage of every species in the plot; - Maximum height of the vegetation, measured in the centre of each subplot, and in the plot centre; - DBH of each tree with more than 10 cm DBH (only in plots with forests or woodlands); - Number of seedlings and saplings in 2 m x 2 m quadrats placed at the corners of the 100 m2 plots (only in plots with forests or woodlands). For bryophytes, we sampled a total of 36 (2 m x 2 m) quadrats and 615 microplots.

Between June 25th and July 5th 2024, we resampled 24 permanent plots, placed and first sampled in 2013, at 12 altitudinal levels (from 0 to 2200 m a.s.l.). We resampled a total of 24 (10 m x 10 m) plots (divided in four, 25 m2, subplots). For vascular plants, we measured: - Percent coverage of every species in the plot; - Maximum height of the vegetation, measured in the centre of each subplot, and in the plot centre; - DBH of each tree with more than 10 cm DBH (only in plots with forests or woodlands); - Number of seedlings and saplings in 2 m x 2 m quadrats placed at the corners of the 100 m2 plots (only in plots with forests or woodlands). For bryophytes, a total of 72 (2 m x 2 m) quadrats and 951 microplots were sampled.
